Administrator's Welcome

It is my absolute pleasure to welcome you to our vibrant community. As the Administrator, I have the distinct privilege of witnessing the "sunrise" of our students' journeys every single day. Whether your child is joining us as a tiny explorer in our nursery or a confident leader in the prep school, they are entering a place where they are truly known, valued, and celebrated.

At Sunrise School, we believe that education is about more than just academic milestones; it is about kindling a lifelong love for discovery. We have built a sanctuary of learning where children feel safe to take risks, ask big questions, and develop the resilience needed for the world beyond our gates.

My door is always open to our parents and pupils. We view ourselves as partners in your child’s growth, working together to ensure their school years are filled with joy, friendship, and profound achievement.

Warmest regards,

Mrs. Ben Kalio Administrator, Sunrise School
