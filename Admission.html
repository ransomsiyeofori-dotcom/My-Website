Admissions Welcome

Sunrise School is a leading non-selective independent day school dedicated to nurturing children from early years through their formative academic stages. We welcome pupils across all year groups, with flexible entry available throughout the academic year.

Families seeking a nurturing, high-standard learning environment choose Sunrise School for its excellent facilities, exceptionally caring atmosphere, and well-rounded educational approach.

At Sunrise School, we are committed to developing confident, capable and compassionate learners who are prepared to thrive both academically and personally.
